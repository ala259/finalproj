About the venue

Fiera Milano has been organising, hosting and managing international events in Italy and worldwide since 1920. The fieramilano exhibition centre offers to visitors a complete range of services that stand out for their quality, variety and efficiency. 84 refreshment points with more than 10,000 seats. ATM, the chemist, the news-stand, the hardware store and the prayer room are just some of the services located inside the Service Centre where it is also possible to find a first aid point. Outside the district, there are over 10,000 parking spaces available.
